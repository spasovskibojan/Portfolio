Early Life and Education

Bojan Spasovski was born on May 24, 2004, in Vinica, Macedonia. He completed his elementary education at "Slavco Stojmenski" and his secondary education at "Vanco Prke", both located in Vinica. Currently, Bojan is pursuing his academic journey at the Faculty of Information Sciences and Computer Engineering (FINKI) in Skopje, where he is enrolled in regular studies.

Passion for Informatics

Bojan's interest in informatics ignited in the 7th grade when he started exploring programming languages such as SCRATCH and Small Basic. His enthusiasm for technology deepened in the 9th grade when he undertook a robotics project involving the LEGO EV3 Robot. This early exposure laid a strong foundation for his future endeavors in computer science.

Technical Skills and Ambitions

During secondary education, Bojan dived into C++ programming and actively participated in regional and state informatics competitions through the MENDO platform. These experiences not only honed his skills in algorithms and data structures but also solidified his decision to pursue programming at FINKI. Furthermore, expanded his knowledge by completing courses in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Git, Bootstrap, and jQuery, gaining proficiency in front-end web technologies. With great ambitions and a clear vision, he aims to have a successful career path as a programmer and ultimately as an engineer.
