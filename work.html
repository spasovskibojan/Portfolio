Projects

Ministry of Finance Website

I created a fully responsive website for Ministry of Finance of Republic of Macedonia using React and Firebase. The site showcases the info about the ministry as well as the latest news. It allows you to choose which language you want to use (mk/en). The coolest feature is the authentication (you can sign in/up) and add new job listings in the job listings part as well as seeing the listings that others have created.

Educational Quiz

I developed an engaging Educational Quiz website focused on natural science subjects like Geography, Biology, Maths, Chemistry, and Physics. Users can enter their name to personalize their experience and attempt a 20-question quiz. At the end, they receive an award page displaying their score and the option to review the correct answers. This project showcases my skills in front-end web development, user experience design, and educational technology, providing a valuable tool for students to enhance their knowledge in a fun and interactive way.
